The rationale behind the TrustedCoin wallet is that your funds should be safe even if your computer gets compromised (assuming you used a separate computer to generate the backup mnemonic, and both your computers aren't compromised by the same actor).

There are a couple scenarios in which you'll want to bypass TrustedCoin in moving your funds:

  • If TrustedCoin goes out of business
  • A hacker has compromised one of your computers, and is spamming you with attempts at stealing your funds

In these situations, you can use both your mnemonics to move your funds unilaterally.

You'll need to know your redeem script, which you should write down as it might not be available if TrustedCoin were to disappear:

Please make a record of the following redeem script: {{wallet.script}}
